### 3. Product Description
StrideMap is a smart travel planning application that creates personalized, savable walking routes. It optimizes itineraries by combining top-rated locations in a city with personal travel dynamics—such as the number of people in the group, daily step/effort goals, arrival time, and the desired travel vibe (chill/relaxing vs. intensive exploration).

### 4. Product Features
* **Dynamic Time Planning:** Generates an optimized, day-by-day itinerary based on the user's arrival time and length of stay.
* **Travel Mode (Vibe Check):** Adjusts route intensity and location selection based on the user's preference for a "Chill" or "Intensive" exploration experience.
* **Group Dynamics Algorithm:** Calculates a realistic timeline that accounts for inevitable delays, rest stops, and dining times depending on the size of the travel group.
* **Effort and Step Optimization:** Adapts walking distances and navigation based on the user's daily step goal and preferred walking pace.
* **Integrated Map:** Displays the generated route sequentially on an interactive map while providing bite-sized information about the landmarks.

### 5. Target Audience
* Independent travelers who love exploring new cities on foot and experiencing the urban atmosphere.
* Students and tourists looking to maximize their experience within a limited timeframe.
* Users traveling in larger groups who need to optimize gathering times and manage break schedules efficiently.
